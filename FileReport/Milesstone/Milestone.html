5.2. Thông tin về project¶

Bi ton nhm mun gii quyt

Trong ch ny, nhm s gii quyt bi ton s dng Twitter API truy cp v tng tc vi d liu t Twitter, c th l thu thp cc tweet, thng tin ngi dng, xu hng (trends) v cc d liu khc lin quan n hot ng trn Twitter. Mc tiu cui cng l thu thp v x l d liu lin quan n ch Black Friday, thng l cc bi qung co, xy dng mt m hnh hc su (Deep Learning) vi mng n-ron hi tip di hn (LSTM - Long Short-Term Memory).

Tp d liu

D liu thu nhp t Twitter

D liu nhn c t Twitter l mt JSON object cha cc thng tin chi tit v mt tweet bao gm cc siu d liu lin quan n tweet, video v thng tin v ngi dng.

D liu training model

Sau khi thu thp c file JSON cha d liu Tweets, vic tin x l l mt bc quan trng chun b d liu cho cc bc tip theo. Trong trng hp ny, mc tiu l ch gi li ni dung ca on Tweets (thuc tnh text) v chuyn i d liu sang nh dng file .txt d dng s dng trong vic training m hnh.

output ca bi ton

  • Mc tiu cui cng ca bi ton ny l xy dng mt m hnh LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) c kh nng t ng sinh vn bn da trn cc bi vit c thu thp t Twitter lin quan n Black Friday. Sau khi m hnh c hun luyn vi d liu tweet, output ca m hnh s l kh nng to ra cc bi vit (tweets) c ni dung tng t, mang m tnh cht ca cc chin dch qung co hoc khuyn mi trong ma Black Friday.

Cch nh gi kt qu

  • So snh loss cui cng: Xem kt qu loss ca epoch cui cng (epoch 63) v so vi cc epoch trc nh gi s ci thin. Nu khng c ci thin ng k, c th m hnh hc y cc c trng ca d liu.

5.3. Giải pháp¶

  • Trong bi ton ny, nhm khng s dng API chnh thc ca Twitter do chnh sch thay i, yu cu phi tr thm ph. Thay vo , nhm la chn RapidAPI, mt nn tng cung cp API mng x hi cho ngi dng, gip truy cp vo d liu t Twitter m khng phi tr chi ph cao. Qu trnh thu thp d liu s c t ng ha thng qua Apache NiFi, mt cng c mnh m xy dng cc workflow x l d liu.

  • D liu thu thp c s c gi ti Apache Kafka thc hin streaming, gip duy tr s lin kt gia cc h thng v m bo tnh sn sng cao cho vic x l d liu theo thi gian thc. Ti y, d liu s c lu tr v truyn ti mt cch linh hot, h tr vic truy xut d dng trong cc giai on sau ca quy trnh.

  • Sau khi d liu c thu thp, n s c x l v chuyn i nh dng t JSON sang TXT thun tin cho vic hun luyn m hnh. Qu trnh ny s c thc hin bi Apache Spark,cc thuc tnh khng cn thit s c loi b ch gi li phn vn bn tweet (text), gip d liu sch v d s dng cho cc bc tip theo.

  • M hnh chnh c nhm s dng x l d liu ny l LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory), mt loi mng n-ron hi tip (RNN) c bit hiu qu trong cc bi ton c tnh chui, nh phn tch vn bn hay to ra vn bn t ng. M hnh ny s gip hc c cc mi quan h gia cc t trong vn bn tweet v s dng kin thc ny sinh ra cc on vn bn mi tng t.

  • Cu trc m hnh LSTM trong bi ton bao gm hai lp LSTM, mi lp c 256 n v, kt hp vi lp Dropout trnh overfitting v tng kh nng tng qut cho m hnh. u ra ca m hnh s l mt lp Dense vi hm kch hot softmax, gip d on t tip theo trong chui vn bn, t sinh ra cc cu hon chnh.

  • Qu trnh hun luyn m hnh s dng categorical crossentropy lm hm mt mt, ph hp vi bi ton phn loi a lp. Adam optimizer s c s dng ti u ha qu trnh hun luyn. M hnh s c lu li mi khi c s ci thin v loss, nh vo ModelCheckpoint.

Thut ton ny c bit ph hp trong bi ton x l ngn ng t nhin (NLP), ni ng cnh ng vai tr quan trng hiu v to ra chui vn bn c ngha. Trong trng hp ny, LSTM khng ch hc cch d on k t tip theo m cn m bo tnh lin kt gia cc k t sinh ra mt vn bn hon chnh, t nhin.

5.4. Kết quả sơ bộ¶

  • D n ny chng minh tim nng mnh m ca cng ngh Text Generation trong vic t ng ha quy trnh to v ng ni dung qung co trn mng x hi. Bng cch tch hp cc cng c tin tin nh Apache NiFi, Apache Kafka, Apache Spark, v cc m hnh hc su nh LSTM, h thng khng ch gip tit kim thi gian m cn tng cng kh nng tip cn khch hng mt cch linh hot v hiu qu. D liu thu thp t Twitter thng qua API ca RapidAPI c x l v chuyn i, sau c s dng hun luyn m hnh, gip to ra cc bi vit qung co ph hp, h tr ti u ha chin lc tip th, tng cng hiu qu qung co v tit kim ngun lc.
  • Kt qu thc nghim cho thy m hnh LSTM c kh nng to ra cc bi ng t ng vi ni dung ph hp v lin quan n ch Black Friday. iu ny khng ch ci thin quy trnh sn xut ni dung m cn gip gim thiu chi ph v thi gian cn thit to ra cc bi vit qung co.
  • Tuy nhin, d n cng i mt vi mt s thch thc quan trng, bao gm vic nng cao cht lng ni dung m m hnh to ra. Mc d m hnh c th to ra cc bi vit tng i mch lc, nhng vn cn mt s vn cn ci thin. c bit, vic m bo tnh chnh xc, sng to v t nhin trong cc bi ng vn l mt thch thc. M hnh i khi gp kh khn trong vic to ra cc ni dung khng ch hp l m cn c tnh ng ngha cao v d hiu i vi ngi c. Cn c thm cc ci tin trong vic ti u ha m hnh, chng hn nh p dng cc k thut iu chnh siu tham s hoc s dng cc m hnh hc su mnh m hn nh Transformer nng cao kh nng sinh ng ngha ca vn bn.
